Replay is a simple tool that takes a podcast feed and rewinds it to the beginning, so episode one is sent to your device today.

Subsequent episodes are then sent to your device at the release cadence the creator intended. So if you subscribe to a daily podcast, you’ll get new episodes each day. Subscribe to a weekly podcast, and they’ll arrive weekly.

This tool doesn’t re-host content. The only thing we tamper with is the publication date for each episode.
